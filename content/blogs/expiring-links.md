# Expiring Links: Time-Limited Links for Maximum Impact

## What are Expiring Links?

Expiring Links are temporary URLs that automatically stop working after a specified date and time. Create urgency, run time-limited promotions, and maintain control over when your content is accessible - all with a single expiring short link.

## Why Use Expiring Links?

### 1. **Create Urgency**
- Drive immediate action with deadlines
- Increase conversion rates through scarcity
- Perfect for limited-time offers
- Encourage faster decision-making

### 2. **Maintain Security**
- Share temporary access to sensitive content
- Automatically revoke access after expiration
- Control distribution of exclusive materials
- Prevent long-term link sharing

### 3. **Campaign Management**
- End promotions automatically
- No need to manually disable links
- Clean campaign boundaries
- Prevent confusion after offers end

## How to Create an Expiring Link

1. **Enter Your URL**: Paste the destination link
2. **Set Expiration Date & Time**: Choose when the link should expire
3. **Configure Expired Behavior**:
   - Show custom expiration message
   - Redirect to another URL
   - Display countdown timer (before expiration)
4. **Share Your Link**: Link works until the specified time

## Expiration Options

### Countdown Timer (Before Expiration)
Display a live countdown on the landing page:
- "This link expires in 5 hours, 32 minutes"
- Builds urgency and FOMO
- Customizable design

### Custom Expiration Message
When link expires, show:
- Branded message with your logo
- Explanation of why link expired
- Call-to-action to current offerings
- Contact information

### Redirect After Expiration
Automatically send users to:
- Your homepage
- A "sorry you missed it" page
- Current promotions
- Alternative offerings

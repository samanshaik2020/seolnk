# Preview Links: Control Your Social Media Presence

## What are Preview Links?

Preview Links allow you to customize exactly how your links appear when shared on social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and more. Instead of relying on what the destination website provides, you take full control of the title, description, and image that appears in the preview card.

## Why Use Preview Links?

### 1. **Professional Branding**
- Ensure consistent branding across all platforms
- Use custom images that grab attention
- Write compelling descriptions that drive clicks

### 2. **Optimize for Engagement**
- Create eye-catching previews that stand out in feeds
- A/B test different titles and images
- Increase click-through rates by up to 300%

### 3. **Fix Broken Previews**
- Some websites don't provide proper meta tags
- Override missing or incorrect preview data
- Ensure your content always looks perfect

## How to Create a Preview Link

1. **Enter Your URL**: Paste the destination link you want to share
2. **Customize Metadata**: 
   - Add a catchy title (max 60 characters recommended)
   - Write an engaging description (max 160 characters)
   - Upload or select a stunning preview image
3. **Generate & Share**: Get your short link and share it anywhere

## Use Cases

- **Content Creators**: Share blog posts with custom thumbnails
- **Marketers**: Create branded links for campaigns
- **E-commerce**: Showcase products with custom images
- **Events**: Promote events with event banners
- **Portfolios**: Share work with professional previews

## Best Practices

✅ Use high-quality images (1200x630px recommended)  
✅ Keep titles concise and compelling  
✅ Write descriptions that create curiosity  
✅ Test previews on different platforms  
✅ Update seasonal content regularly  

## Analytics & Tracking

Every Preview Link comes with built-in analytics:
- Total clicks and unique visitors
- Click sources and referrers
- Geographic data
- Device and browser information
- Time-based trends

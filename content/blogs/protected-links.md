# Protected Links: Secure Your Content with Password Protection

## What are Protected Links?

Protected Links are password-secured URLs that require authentication before granting access to the destination. Share exclusive content, protect sensitive information, and control who can access your links - all with a simple password layer.

## Why Use Protected Links?

### 1. **Content Security**
- Restrict access to authorized users only
- Protect sensitive documents and files
- Share private resources securely
- Control distribution of exclusive content

### 2. **Exclusivity & VIP Access**
- Create members-only content
- Provide early access to select groups
- Build community with insider content
- Reward loyal customers or subscribers

### 3. **Simple Access Control**
- No complex login systems required
- One password for easy sharing
- Change or revoke access anytime
- Track who's accessing your content

## How to Create a Protected Link

1. **Enter Destination URL**: The link you want to protect
2. **Set Your Password**: Choose a secure password
3. **Customize Access Page** (Optional):
   - Add custom message or instructions
   - Brand with your logo and colors
   - Include hint or contact information
4. **Share Link + Password**: Distribute separately for maximum security

## Security Levels

### Basic Protection
- Single password for all users
- Simple setup and sharing
- **Best for**: Content with moderate sensitivity

### Enhanced Protection
- Multiple passwords for different groups
- Access logging enabled
- Custom expiration per password
- **Best for**: Business and professional use

### Maximum Protection
- Regularly rotated passwords
- IP restriction options
- Two-step verification (coming soon)
- **Best for**: Highly sensitive content

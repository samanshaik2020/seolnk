# Rotator Links: Smart Link Distribution Made Easy

## What are Rotator Links?

Rotator Links (also known as Link Rotators or Round-Robin Links) are intelligent URLs that automatically distribute clicks across multiple destination URLs. One short link, multiple destinations - perfect for A/B testing, load balancing, and maximizing conversions.

## Why Use Rotator Links?

### 1. **A/B Testing Made Simple**
- Test multiple landing pages simultaneously
- Compare performance without complex setups
- Split traffic evenly or use weighted distribution
- Make data-driven decisions

### 2. **Load Distribution**
- Prevent server overload during campaigns
- Distribute affiliate traffic across multiple links
- Balance traffic across multiple stores or regions

### 3. **Maximize Conversions**
- Automatically send traffic to the best-performing destination
- Test different offers, copy, or designs
- Optimize campaigns in real-time

## How Rotator Links Work

1. **Add Multiple URLs**: Enter 2 or more destination URLs
2. **Set Weights** (Optional): Control traffic distribution percentage
3. **Choose Strategy**: 
   - **Round Robin**: Equal distribution, rotates sequentially
   - **Random**: Random selection from available URLs
   - **Weighted**: Custom percentage for each URL
4. **Share One Link**: Clicks automatically route to different destinations

## Use Cases

### Marketing & Advertising
- **A/B Testing**: Compare landing page designs
- **Regional Targeting**: Route to location-specific pages

### E-commerce
- **Inventory Management**: Route to stores with stock

### Content Distribution
- **Blog Posts**: Test different headlines
- **Videos**: Rotate between platforms (YouTube, Vimeo)

- **Forms**: Balance form submissions
